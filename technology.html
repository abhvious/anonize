How it Works.

1. Register the Anonize App.

Download the Anonize App (freely available for iPhone and Android). You will be asked to enter your email and a verification code that is sent to your email. You have now completed the Anonize registration, and you never need to complete it again (unless your loose your phone).

2. Fill out a survey.

Standard online surveys either send participants a personalized link or require log-in. In both cases, your identity is linked to your responses. In Anonize, all participants receive the same link to a public webpage (like this one), and are not required to identify themselves. You fill out the survey using a webbrowser.

3. Submit using the Anonize app.

If you completed the survey on your phone, you will be directly taken to the Anonize app to anonymously submit your response. If you completed the survey on you computer, a QR code appears. Scan this QR code with the Anonize app to anonymously submit the response.

Under the Hood.



User Registration

When you register your Anonize app, the app receives in return a cryptographic "master token". Think of this master token as a stamp. This stamp can be used to mint "digital coins", which will be used to submit surveys.


Only Authorized Users Can Submit

Using their master token, a user can mint one and only one coin for any survey they are authorized for. On the other hand, no user can forge a coin for surveys they are not authorized for. Anyone can verify whether a coin is "valid" for a particular survey.


Coins are Unlinkable

Although anyone can verify that the coin minted by a user is valid (that is, it corresponds to a user that is authorized to submit in the survey) there is absolutely no link between the user and the coin. The coins can therefore be used to replace user names when submitting a survey.
