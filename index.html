Introducing ANONIZE.


Traditional Web Surveys are Not Anonymous

Traditional survey system on the web claim that your response is anonymous, but in reality, they keep a database that maintains an explicit link between you and your response. They need this to make sure you only respond ONCE. You must trust them to maintain your anonymity, you must trust that they never get hacked.

This is a DANGEROUS principle.

A Cryptographic Solution

ANONIZE is a new cryptographic technology that ensures only authorized users can respond (exactly once), while unconditionally guaranteeing anonymity: even if our servers are fully compromised, and all data is leaked, a link between users and their responses cannot be found.

Using ANONIZE

Anyone can create a survey by specifying a set of authorized user emails. The survey is posted on a public webpage (like this one) that can be sent to all users. Users need to download and register their Anonize App (iPhone and Android available) and can then use this app to anonymously submit survey responses.
